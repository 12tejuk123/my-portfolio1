CognitoLearn

Comprehensive AI Study & Abroad Prep Platform.

Overview

A complete AI-based study and abroad preparation assistant designed specifically for students. It unifies academic preparation with the complex process of applying to foreign universities in one platform.

Key Features

  • AI SOP (Statement of Purpose) Generator
  • AI Resume Builder tailored for universities
  • Smart university shortlisting based on profile
  • Exam prep resources (DSA, Coding, Maths)
  • Deadline tracker and study planner
  • Notes and previous year paper generator

Technologies Used

AI / GPT Models Firebase Auth HTML/CSS/JS Python Backend

Purpose

To streamline the chaotic journey of academic prep and study abroad planning into a single, intelligent application.
