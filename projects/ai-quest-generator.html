AI Quest Generator

An intelligent system generating dynamic quests for RPGs and creative writing.

Overview

An AI-powered application designed to generate fantasy, adventure, and story quests for games, novels, or Dungeons & Dragons campaigns. It leverages advanced language models to create unique and immersive storylines instantly.

Technologies Used

OpenAI API HTML/CSS/JS Python Firebase

Purpose

To assist gamers, dungeon masters, writers, and developers by overcoming writer's block and providing instant, high-quality creative content.
