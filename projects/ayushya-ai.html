Ayushya AI

Personal AI Doctor assistant for symptom analysis and health guidance.

Overview

Ayushya AI is a smart health assistant where users can describe their symptoms, and the AI analyzes the input to suggest possible conditions and home remedies. It serves as a first-response medical guide for immediate assistance.

Key Features

  • AI diagnosis using advanced symptom analysis
  • Medicine and home remedy suggestions
  • Chatbot-style interactive medical assistant
  • Emergency alert information
  • Daily health tips and wellness tracking

Technologies Used

AI / NLP Models Python Medical Datasets HTML/CSS/JS

Purpose

To provide the public with quick, accessible primary health guidance and reduce anxiety during medical uncertainty.
