Artificial Large Intestine

Biomedical innovation restoring functionality for patients post-surgery.

Overview

A biomedical innovation model designed to replicate and replace the functionality of the large intestine for patients who have undergone major surgery. This system aims to restore quality of life through advanced biological modeling and waste processing.

Key Features

  • Artificial waste processing algorithms
  • Fluid and nutrient absorption regulation
  • Advanced bacterial filtration system
  • Integrated safety valves for pressure management

Technologies & Domains

Biomedical Engineering System Modeling Prototyping Healthcare Tech

Purpose

To drastically improve the quality of life and recovery outcomes for patients coping with the loss of large intestine functionality.
