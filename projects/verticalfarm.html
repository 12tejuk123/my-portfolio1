Smart Vertical Farming

Winner of DIPEX Competition - AI & IoT powered sustainable agriculture.

Overview

An award-winning smart vertical farming system that integrates AI, IoT, and renewable energy to optimize crop growth. It ensures sustainable agriculture with minimal human intervention.

Key Features

  • Automated irrigation using moisture sensors
  • Real-time temperature & humidity monitoring
  • AI-driven crop health analysis
  • Nutrient and water requirement prediction
  • Solar-powered independent setup

Technologies Used

Arduino / ESP32 IoT Sensors Python AI Solar Tech

Purpose

To promote sustainable, high-yield agriculture in urban environments with low resource consumption.
