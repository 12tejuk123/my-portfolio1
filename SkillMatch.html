SkillMatch AI

Intelligent job-skills matching and career guidance system.

Overview

SkillMatch is a dynamic system that bridges the gap between job seekers and employers. It analyzes user skills using AI and matches them to suitable career roles with a high degree of accuracy.

Key Features

  • AI-driven skill analysis and profiling
  • Career compatibility scoring
  • Automated resume improvement suggestions
  • Smart job role recommendations

Technologies Used

AI / NLP Models Python Backend HTML/CSS/JS Data Analytics

Purpose

To empower students and job seekers in finding their ideal career path by providing data-driven insights and recommendations.
